Pasta Carbonara

This creamy dish of pasta in a luxurious sauce of bacon, egg and cheese is an easy-to-make treat. Adding lots of good, fresh black pepper will really add to the brightness of the dish and make the other ingredients "pop."

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound of bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 pound pasta, cooked al dente
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a large pan over medium heat until crisp
  2. Remove the bacon from the pan, and pour off all but 2 tablespoons of the rendered fat
  3. Add the garlic and black pepper to the pan and cook until fragrant, just a minute or so
  4. Add the bacon and pasta back to the pan and cook for another minute
  5. Take the pan off the heat and add the eggs and cheese, stir quickly so the eggs thicken but do not scramble
